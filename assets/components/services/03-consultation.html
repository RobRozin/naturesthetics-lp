Comprehensive Consultation

We start with an in-depth consultation to fully understand your skin type, main concerns, and lifestyle. This allows us to design a skincare plan that is uniquely suited to your needs, creating the perfect foundation for a healthier, radiant complexion.

What’s included in the consultation?

1.
Skin Analysis

Determining your skin type and condition, assessing your skin according to the Fitzpatrick scale, identifying any skin concerns, and medical history collection.

2.
Analysis of Your Current Skincare Routine

I will review each product you use, explaining which products to keep in your routine and which ones to replace, along with the reasons why.

3.
Selection of Effective and Safe Skincare Products
  • Makeup remover
  • Cleanser
  • Toner
  • Serum
  • Cream
  • Sunscreen
  • Exfoliant
  • Mask
  • Lip balm
  • Eye care product
  • SOS product

When selecting products, your budget preferences are taken into account

Each product will be provided with 1-3 options, including a description of the product’s effects, cost, and a link to the store

4.
Creating an Effective Step-by-Step Skincare Strategy

A personalized skincare plan based on your skin type and concerns, where each product has a specific role to achieve the desired results

5.
Lifestyle Recommendations

As a result of the consultation, you will receive a personalized skincare guide and routine in a PDF file, over 60 pages long.

The Completion time is 5 business days from the payment date.
